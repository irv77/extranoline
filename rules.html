Forum Rules
Unregistered, You must accept the Forum Rules below to be able to use some forum functions.
  1. All posts must be written in English.
  2. Do not post 'Thank You' posts
  3. Do not post mirrors for attachments uploaded by other members
  4. Do not request external mirrors for attachments which you do not have permission to download.
  5. Strive to keep posts clean, friendly, and on topic.
  6. Make sure that you post new threads in the correct section.
  7. Posts unrelated to reverse engineering are discouraged.
  8. Do not spam, harass, or abuse another member via post, e-mail, private message, or any other means.
  9. Creation of a community chat group is strictly forbidden.
  10. Use of the Extranoline name in any manner is forbidden without direct permission from the administration.
  11. Use the Report Post feature only for posts that are in violation of these forum policies.
  12. Have Fun!
For breaking above rules you may be warned/banned appropriately!
